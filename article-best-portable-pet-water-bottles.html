4. How to Train Your Pet to Drink from a Bottle

  1. At home, pour water into the bowl and let your pet drink from it.
  2. Gradually offer water from the bottle spout or bowl.
  3. During walks, stop and offer water every 15‑20 minutes.
  4. If your pet is reluctant, try using the water to wet the bottom of their mouth – most dogs will then lick and drink.

6. What to Avoid

  • Bottles that are hard to clean – bacteria can grow in crevices.
  • Cheap plastic that is not BPA‑free – may contain harmful chemicals.
  • Bottles that leak – can ruin your bag or car.
  • Small capacity for large breeds – 12 oz is not enough for a large dog on a long walk.
