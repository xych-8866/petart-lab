3. How to Use Joint Supplements Effectively

Give the supplement daily – consistency is key. Most dogs show improvement within 4-6 weeks. Pair with a healthy weight and low-impact exercise like swimming or leash walks on soft surfaces. Never give human pain relievers (like ibuprofen) to dogs – they are toxic.

4. When to See the Vet

If your dog doesn't improve after two months of quality supplements, or if they show severe pain (crying, unable to stand), see your veterinarian. They may prescribe prescription anti-inflammatories or recommend physical therapy.
