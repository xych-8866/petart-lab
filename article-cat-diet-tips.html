What to Feed Your Cat: Daily Diet Tips for a Healthy Feline

By PetArt Lab · May 23, 2026

Key Takeaways

  • Choose high-protein food with real meat as the first ingredient
  • Use stainless steel bowls to prevent bacteria
  • Always provide fresh, clean water
  • Never feed your cat dog food—it lacks essential taurine

A healthy diet is the foundation of your cat's well-being. Unlike dogs, cats are obligate carnivores—they must eat meat to survive. Here's what every cat owner should know about feeding their feline friend.

1. Prioritize High-Protein Food

The first ingredient on any cat food label should be a named meat source like chicken, salmon, or turkey. Avoid foods where corn, wheat, or soy appear first. Cats need animal protein to thrive.

2. Choose the Right Bowls

3. Fresh Water Is Essential

Cats naturally have a low thirst drive. Encourage drinking by providing fresh water daily and consider a pet water fountain. Many cats prefer running water.

4. Never Feed Dog Food to Cats

Dog food lacks taurine, an amino acid cats can't produce on their own. Without taurine, cats can develop serious heart and eye problems. Always feed cat-specific food.
