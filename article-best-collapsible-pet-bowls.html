1. Why Choose a Collapsible Bowl?

  • Portable and lightweight – packs easily in a backpack, bag, or pocket.
  • Space‑saving – folds flat when not in use.
  • Durable – made of silicone or BPA‑free plastic.
  • Easy to clean – usually dishwasher‑safe or easy to hand wash.
  • Versatile – suitable for water, dry food, or wet food.

4. How to Choose the Right Size

  • Small (8‑12 oz) – for cats and small dogs (under 15 lbs).
  • Medium (16‑24 oz) – for medium dogs (15‑40 lbs).
  • Large (30‑40 oz) – for large dogs (40‑75 lbs).
  • Extra large (40+ oz) – for very large dogs (75+ lbs) and extended outings.

5. How to Clean and Store

  • Hand wash – with warm, soapy water and a soft brush.
  • Dishwasher safe – if indicated by the manufacturer.
  • Store flat – allow to dry completely before folding to prevent mold.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals – which can degrade silicone.

7. What to Avoid

  • Low‑quality silicone – can tear or retain odors.
  • Bowls without a stable base – tip over easily, causing spills.
  • Hard‑to‑clean designs – can harbor bacteria.
  • Not drying properly – can lead to mold and mildew.
